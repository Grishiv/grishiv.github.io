Synopsis

Haunted by the loss of his parents, Vikram, a seemingly ordinary teenager, grapples with a shocking diagnosis: Split-Personality Disorder. As he struggles to manage his fractured identity, he realises he is being pursued by a vicious detective who could destroy his life. Can Vikram overcome his inner demons or will the monster within destroy everything he holds dear?
